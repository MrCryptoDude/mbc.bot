How Voting Works

Each page post includes three options labeled 🅰️ 🅱️ 🅲️. Since Twitter doesn't allow polls with media attachments, votes are cast through replies and engagement.

The AI reads the results and incorporates the winning direction into the next page. Every vote matters — the story branches based on what the community chooses.

The Lore Master posts automatically every few hours, so the story keeps moving even while you sleep. 🌙
