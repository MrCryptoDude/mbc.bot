One Post = One Page

Every time the AI Lore Master posts to Twitter, that's one page in the ongoing manga series. Each page advances the story based on the community's previous vote.

After a set number of pages, the chapter wraps up and a new chapter arc begins. The result is a collaboratively written manga — powered by AI art and community decisions.

How Voting Works

Each page post includes three options labeled 🅰️ 🅱️ 🅲️. Since Twitter doesn't allow polls with media attachments, votes are cast through replies and engagement.

The AI reads the results and incorporates the winning direction into the next page. Every vote matters — the story branches based on what the community chooses.

The Lore Master posts automatically every few hours, so the story keeps moving even while you sleep. Come back, catch up on pages, and cast your vote on the latest one.
