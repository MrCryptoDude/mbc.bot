// Gameplay

How It Works

Enemies march toward your tree from the right. You control the squirrel, switching weapons, firing at enemies, and building combos. Every 3 waves, pick a roguelike upgrade card to power up your build. Survive as long as you can.

Combo System 🔥

Chain kills without missing to build your combo meter. Hit milestones at 150, 250, 350, 450, and 500 to earn temporary damage and fire rate buffs. At peak combo, your squirrel gets a DBZ-style power-up aura. Miss a shot and lose it all.

Roguelike Upgrades 🃏

Every 3 waves, choose from randomized upgrade cards. Stack fire rate, unlock split-shot turrets, boost ability damage, or improve your weapons. Each run plays differently depending on what you pick.
